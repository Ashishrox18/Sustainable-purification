    <section id="about" class="section container">
      <h3>Problem</h3>
      <p>Untreated industrial wastewater and excessive chemical fertilizer use are contaminating farmland, reducing yields, and damaging ecosystems. Farmers lack affordable tools for clean water and precise resource allocation.</p>

      <h3>Objective</h3>
      <p>Create a low-cost, circular system that purifies wastewater using algae, converts biomass into fertilizer, and uses IoT + AI to optimize water and nutrient allocation.</p>
    </section>
